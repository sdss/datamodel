## Basic Information
Robust data-driven metallicities for 120 million stars from Gaia XP spectra. See Andrae et al. (2023).

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_xpfeh_gaia_dr3-[NUM].[FTYPE] with FTYPE='fits', V_TARG='2.0.0', and NUM=1..16 for DR20.
