## Basic Information
Gaia DR2 parameters for AAVSO cataclysmic variables from cataclysmic (explosive and nova-like) variables (N, NA, NB, NC, NL, NR, SN, SNI, SNII, UG, UGSS, UGSU, UGZ, ZAND). VSX catalog downloaded in summer 2019 and then manually pruned.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_cataclysmic_variables.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.

### Releases
