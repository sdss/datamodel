## Basic Information
Catalog of pre-main-sequence stars derived from Gaia DR2 and 2MASS from McBride et al. (2021), their Table 4.

For complete details, see the original paper: <a href="https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/ac2432">https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-3881/ac2432</a>

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_sagitta.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='fits' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
