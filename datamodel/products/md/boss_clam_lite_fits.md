## Basic Information
BOSS-CLAM is a data driven method that maps labels to NMF basis weights to forward model continuum normalized BOSS spectra. This model was training with labels from ASPCAP, BOSS-MINESweeper, wide binaries and a validation set of hot stars. This is the lite version of the VAC includes the inferened stellar parameters from all mwmStar BOSS spectra with snr > 10, a series of flags to clean the dataset, and a subset of meta-data from mwmAllStar.
