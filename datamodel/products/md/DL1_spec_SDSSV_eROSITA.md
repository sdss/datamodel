## Basic Information
Data Level 1 contains the data shared among the collaborations of SDSS and eROSITA, with optical and X-ray information of sources that were detected with eROSITA and followed-up with SDSS

### Naming Convention
$DL1_SDSS_EROSITA/[VERS]/DL1_spec_SDSSV_eROSITA_[ERASS]-[VERS].fits or $DL1_SDSS_EROSITA/[VERS]/DL1_spec_SDSSV_eROSITA_[ERASS]_allepoch-[VERS].fits

### Releases
DR19, DR20

### Enviroments
DL1_SDSS_EROSITA

### Approximate Size
10 MB

### File Type
FITS

### Generated by Product
DL1_SDSS_EROSITA

### Is a VAC
True

### Data Level
