## Basic Information
This VAC provides measurements of the Na I D (5889.95/5895.92 Å) interstellar absorption doublet and V-band dust extinction (A_V, assuming R_V = 3.1) for 1,373,392 stellar sightlines observed with the BOSS spectrograph as part of the SDSS-V Milky Way Mapper program. Equivalent widths and extinctions are derived from spectral fits via ISM-cleaned MaStar spectra; uncertainties and fit quality (reduced chi^2) are reported for each source. See McQuaid et al. (2026) for full methodology.
