## Basic Information
The table contains the cameras at the observatory. The three APOGEE chips are treated as one camera, while the separate blue channel and red channel BOSS chips are treated separately. APO version.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_opsdb_apo_camera.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='fits' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
