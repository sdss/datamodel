## Basic Information
The 2021 release of the XMM OM Serendipitous Ultraviolet Source Survey (XMM-SUSS5.0) Catalog.

For full details, please see <a href="https://heasarc.gsfc.nasa.gov/W3Browse/all/xmmomsuob.html">https://heasarc.gsfc.nasa.gov/W3Browse/all/xmmomsuob.html</a> and the original catalog paper (Page et al. 2012; <a href="https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/426/2/903/976665)">https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/426/2/903/976665)</a> and the 2021 update <a href="https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021yCat.2370">https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021yCat.2370</a>....0P/abstract.
