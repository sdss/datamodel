## Basic Information
BOSS-CLAM is a data driven method that maps labels to NMF basis weights to forward model continuum normalized BOSS spectra. This model was training with labels from ASPCAP, BOSS-MINESweeper, wide binaries and a validation set of hot stars. This is the lite version of the VAC includes the inferened stellar parameters from all mwmStar BOSS spectra with snr > 10, a series of flags to clean the dataset, and a subset of meta-data from mwmAllStar.

### clam/clam_flags

**Name:** clam/clam_flags\
**Parent:**  /clam\
**Description:** Bitmask indicating potentially unreliable or out-of-distribution BOSS-CLAM results. Flags include:
  - IN_WD (0): likely white dwarf in mwm_wd not represented in the training set
  - IN_YSO (1): likely young stellar object in mwm_yso not represented in the training set
  - IN_OB (2): likely hot star in mwm_ob with unreliable [Fe/H] or [alpha/M] training coverage
  - FE_H_ALPHA_SUSPECT (3): Teff > 6500 K, where [Fe/H] and [alpha/M] are poorly constrained
  - BAD_SPEC_FIT (4): reduced chi^2 > 1 indicating poor forward-model fit
  - LOW_MASS_SUSPECT (5): Teff < 4000 K and [Fe/H] < -1 in sparsely sampled low-mass regime
  - FE_H_ALPHA_CORR (6): atypical correlation between [Fe/H], [alpha/M], and Teff
  - TEFF_LOGG_CORR (7): unphysical negative correlation between Teff and logg
  - LOW_MASS_TEFF_LOGG_CORR (8): weak Teff-logg correlation in low-mass stars (Teff < 4200 K)
  - EXTREME_CORR (9): extreme correlations involving [alpha/M]
  - ALTER_CORR (10): alternating correlation structure relative to the bulk population
\
**Type:** dataset



**Ndim:** 1\
**Shape:** (1708214,)\
**Size:** 1708214\
**Dtype:** int64\
**Nbytes:** 13665712
