## Basic Information
Tycho-2 catalog.

For complete details, please see the original Tycho-2 documentation from Hog et al (2020) <a href="https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2000A%26A">https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2000A%26A</a>...355L..27H/abstract and <a href="https://www.cosmos.esa.int/web/hipparcos/tycho-2">https://www.cosmos.esa.int/web/hipparcos/tycho-2</a>
