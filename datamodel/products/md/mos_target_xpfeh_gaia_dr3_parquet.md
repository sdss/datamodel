## Basic Information
Robust data-driven metallicities for 120 million stars from Gaia XP spectra. See Andrae et al. (2023).

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_xpfeh_gaia_dr3.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
