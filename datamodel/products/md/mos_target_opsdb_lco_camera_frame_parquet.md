## Basic Information
The table contains signal-to-noise estimates for each 'camera', for each exposure. APOGEE is treated as one camera, while R1/2 and B1/2 are treated separately. LCO version.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_opsdb_lco_camera_frame.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
