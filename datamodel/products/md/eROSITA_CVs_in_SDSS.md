## Basic Information
Cataclysmic variable binaries confirmed by screening and visual inspection of SDSS-V spectra of eROSITA eRASS1 and eRASS:3 X-ray sources, with optical data from Gaia DR3 and X-ray data from eRASS:3

### Naming Convention
$MWM_WHITEDWARF/eROSITA_CVs/eROSITA_CVs_in_SDSS-[V_VAC].fits where V_VAC=1.0.0 in DR20.

### Releases
DR20

### Enviroments
MWM_WHITEDWARF

### Approximate Size
177 KB

### File Type
FITS

### Generated by Product
Jaco Brink and collaborators within eROSITA and SDSS-V this product.

### Is a VAC
True

### Data Level
3.3.0
