## Basic Information
The Heraklion Extragalactic Catalogue (HECATE). For more details, refer to Kovlakas et al. 2021 (<a href="https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/506/2/1896/6309318)">https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/506/2/1896/6309318)</a>.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_hecate_1_1.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
