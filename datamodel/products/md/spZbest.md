## Basic Information
The spZbest files contain the spectroscopic classifications and redshifts from the idlspec1d step of the spectro pipeline.  This page is an updated summary of the <a href="http://spectro.astro.princeton.edu/#dm_spzbest">SDSS2 spZbest
data model</a> .

### Naming Convention
Filenames have the form <i>spZbest-pppp-mmmmm.fits</i> , where <i>pppp</i> is a 4-digit plate number, and <i>mmmmm</i> is a 5-digit MJD.

### Releases
DR9, DR10, DR12, DR11, DR13, DR14, DR15, DR16, DR17

### Enviroments
BOSS_SPECTRO_REDUX

### File Type

### Generated by Product
