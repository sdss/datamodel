## Basic Information
manga-firefly-globalprop-v3_1_1-miles.fits is one of the MaNGA FIREFLY catalogues for the 17th data release of the Sloan Digital Sky Survey. It is a light-weight version of the MaNGA FIREFLY VAC reduced to only the global stellar population parameters. For spatially resolved parameters, please refer to the main VAC. This file contains analysis for 10735 datacubes including 10010 unique galaxies from the MaNGA survey, providing global stellar population parameters such as gradients and values at the center and at 1 Re. For DR17, the version of FIREFLY used was FIREFLY_VER=v1_0_1, and this was run on reduced and analysed data from MANGADRP_VER=v3_1_1 and MANGADAP_VER=v3_1_0. This VAC is provided in two versions: The first version employs the stellar population models of ​Maraston & Stromback (2011) based on the ​ ​MILES stellar library (Sánchez-Blázquez et al. 2006). The second version uses the ​​ ​MaStar models described in ​ ​Maraston et al. (2020) . Both model libraries assume a Kroupa IMF (Kroupa 2001). We assume a flat cosmology with H 0 = 67.8 km/s/Mpc and Ω m = 0.308 (Planck Collaboration et al. 2016). Compared to the FIREFLY VAC in DR15, the radius in HDU4 is now given in elliptical coordinates and the azmiuth is added, too. The catalogue is available in FITS format at the following link. Please do not forget to cite Neumann et al. (2022) and Goddard et al. (2017) if you use this catalogue in your work. https://data.sdss.org/sas/dr17/manga/spectro/firefly/v3_1_1/manga-firefly-globalprop-v3_1_1-miles.fits https://data.sdss.org/sas/dr17/manga/spectro/firefly/v3_1_1/manga-firefly-globalprop-v3_1_1-mastar.fits Naming Convention filename = manga-firefly-globalprop-MANGADRP_VER-MODELS.fits where MANGADRP_VER=v3_1_1 for DR17 and MODELS either miles or mastar .
