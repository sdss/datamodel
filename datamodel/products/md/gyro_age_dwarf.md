## Basic Information
Gyrochronology ages (rotation based ages) for dwarf stars determined using GPgyro (Lu. et al. 2024) and gyro-interp (Bouma et al. 2023). GPgyro is suitable for stars > 1.5 Gyr, and gyrointerp is suitable for stars < 4 Gyr.

### Naming Convention
$MWM_STELLAR_AGE/gyro_age/gyro_age_dwarf-[V_VAC].fits where V_VAC = 1.0.0 for DR20.

### Releases
DR20

### Enviroments
MWM_STELLAR_AGE

### Approximate Size
641 KB

### File Type
FITS

### Generated by Product
https://github.com/lyx12311/GPgyro, https://github.com/lgbouma/gyro-interp this product.

### Is a VAC
