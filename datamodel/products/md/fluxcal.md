## Basic Information
The fluxcal files store intermediate products derived during the flux calibration procedure.

### Naming Convention
fluxcal-[camera]-[exposure].fits.gz where [camera] is one of the four cameras on the instrument, b1, r1, or b2, r2, for the blue and red cameras on each SDSS spectrograph, and [exposure] is the (zero-padded) 8-digit exposure number.

### Releases
DR13

### Enviroments
MANGA_SPECTRO_REDUX

### Approximate Size
901 KB

### File Type
FITS

### Generated by Product
mangadrp: mdrp_fluxcal.pro

### Is a VAC
