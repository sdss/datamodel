## Basic Information
This catalogue contains photometric stellar parameters for 9+ million stars in common between the SkyMapper survey and Gaia DR2.

See <a href="https://skymapper.anu.edu.au/_data/sm-gaia/">https://skymapper.anu.edu.au/_data/sm-gaia/</a> for details.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_skymapper_gaia-[NUM].[FTYPE] with FTYPE='fits', V_TARG='2.0.0', and NUM=1..2 for DR20.
