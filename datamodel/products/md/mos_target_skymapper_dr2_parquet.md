## Basic Information
Skymapper Data Release 2 photometry.

For detailed descriptions, please see the SkyMapper documentation: <a href="https://skymapper.anu.edu.au/table-browser/">https://skymapper.anu.edu.au/table-browser/</a>

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_skymapper_dr2.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
