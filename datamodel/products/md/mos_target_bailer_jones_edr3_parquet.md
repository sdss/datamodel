## Basic Information
Geometric and photogeometric distances to 1.47 billion stars in Gaia Early Data Release 3 from Bailer-Jones et al. (2021) (see <a href="https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021AJ">https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2021AJ</a>....161..147B/abstract).

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_bailer_jones_edr3.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
