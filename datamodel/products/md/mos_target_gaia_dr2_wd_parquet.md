## Basic Information
White dwarf catalog of high-probablity WDs from Gentile Fusillo (2019) based on Gaia DR2.

See <a href="https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/482/4/4570/5162857">https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/482/4/4570/5162857</a> for more information on the columns.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_gaia_dr2_wd.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
