## Basic Information
We trained Payne models on grids of Turbospectrum_NLTE synthetic spectra: one in LTE and one in NLTE, using departure coefficient grids calculated in MULTI. These are the model spectra that come from our Paynes, after chi-squared fitting the APOGEE DR19 spectra that fall within our training range (according to ASPCAP). In addition to the 15-label Payne, we also fit each spectrum with 9 continuum parameters (2nd order Chebyshev polynomials for each chip), radial velocity and gaussian broadening.

### Naming Convention
$MWM_RGB/apogee_rgb_nlte_abundances/payne4GAIN_modelSpectra-[V_VAC].fits
where V_VAC=1.1.1 for DR20
