## Basic Information
The table contains the cross-match between the mos_target_catalog targets and the mos_target_tycho2 table.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/mos_catalog_to_tycho2-[NUM].fits, where V_TARG=1.0.1 for DR18; and NUM = 1..1 to partition the file into parts

### Releases

### Enviroments
