## Basic Information
Legacy Survey DR80 catalogue derived from 'sweep' catalogues.

For more information see <a href="<a href="https://www.legacysurvey.org/dr8/">https://www.legacysurvey.org/dr10/</a>">https://www.legacysurvey.org/dr8/">https://www.legacysurvey.org/dr10/</a></a>.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_legacy_survey_dr10.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
