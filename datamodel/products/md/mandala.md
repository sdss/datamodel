### HDU1: 
This extension contains the binary table with our results derived with photometric
  (DESI) and spectroscopic (MaNGA) data. For all of them we have assumed the following cosmology: 
  H0 = 70, Omega_M= 0.3, Omega_Lambda = 0.7, unless otherwise is explicitly stated.
  For the photometric results (up to column 54): 
  magnitudes are in AB system. The absolute magnitudes are calculated with the Distance Modulus:
  M = m - 5 * log10(DL), where DL is the Luminosity Distance, which is estimated using theNSAredshifts.
  For the Sersic fits we convolved the PSF with a Moffat function, and used the following
  values of the FWHM in each band: g: 1.22, r: 1.12 and z: 1.10, all in arcsec.
  The spectroscopic results (columns 55-84) were derived from the followingPipe3Ddata products: D4000, age, metallicity, 
  emission line fluxes and equivalent width maps, as well as stellar decompositions. 
  Note that the quantities reported in this VAC may differ from those presented 
in the Pipe3D VAC due to differences in aperture definitions.
