## Basic Information
M dwarf pseudo-continuum from <a href=" https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2025AJ....170..302M/abstract">Medan, Way, et al. (2025)</a>, which can be used to standardize BOSS spectra of M dwarfs. When standardized, the issues in flux calibration in the optical spectra are removed such that M dwarfs of similar stellar parameters should not have comparable spectrum shapes and features. This file only includes meta-data from mwmAllVisit. The flux and pseudo-continua for the spectra are included in the HDF5 file also included with the VAC.
