## Basic Information
AGN identified using information from WISE and Gaia DR2 from the catalog of Shu et al. 2019.

See complete information on the columns in <a href="https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/489/4/4741/5561523">https://academic.oup.com/mnras/article/489/4/4741/5561523</a>

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_gaia_unwise_agn.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
