## Basic Information
The unWISE catalog, containing the positions and fluxes of approximately two billion objects observed by the Wide-field Infrared Survey Explorer (WISE).

For more details, see Schlafly et al. (2019). The original catalogs are hosted at <a href="https://catalog.unwise.me/catalogs.html">https://catalog.unwise.me/catalogs.html</a>

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_unwise.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
