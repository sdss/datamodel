# Data Model: payne4GAIN_summary


Summary table of the LTE and NLTE Payne fits of red giant spectra, as well as aspcap raw values, and NLTE corrected aspcap raw values.

## Basic Information
We trained Payne models on grids of Turbospectrum_NLTE synthetic spectra: one in LTE and one in NLTE,  using departure coefficient grids calculated in MULTI. We chi-squared fit the APOGEE DR19 spectra that fall within our training range (according to ASPCAP) and present the results of our fits. We also perform a polynomial fit to the NLTE-LTE corrections, expecting them to vary smoothly with the stellar parameters, and apply them to the aspcap raw values. The aspcap raw values are also packaged with this VAC, for comparison.
