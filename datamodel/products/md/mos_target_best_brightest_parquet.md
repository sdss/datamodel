## Basic Information
Catalog for the selection of bright metal-poor stars from the method of Schlaufman and Casey (2014): <a href="https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2014ApJ">https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2014ApJ</a>...797...13S/abstract. WISE values from WHICH CATALOG, 2MASS values from 2MASS point source catalog.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_best_brightest.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
