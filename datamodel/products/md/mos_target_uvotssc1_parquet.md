## Basic Information
Version 1.1 of the Swift UVOT Serendipitous Source Catalogue (UVOTSSC).

For full details, please see Page et al. (2015) at <a href="https://pos.sissa.it/233/037">https://pos.sissa.it/233/037</a> and the online documentation at <a href="https://archive.stsci.edu/prepds/uvotssc/">https://archive.stsci.edu/prepds/uvotssc/</a>

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_uvotssc1.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
