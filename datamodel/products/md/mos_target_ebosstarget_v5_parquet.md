## Basic Information
A catalog of targets, selected from SDSS+WISE imaging that were considered for observation in the SDSS-IV/eBOSS project, including spectrophotometric starts and candidate QSOs.

 This catalogue is a data product of the ebosstarget target selection software, version "v5_0".

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_ebosstarget_v5.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
