## Basic Information
This table contains basic information about a configuration of robots loaded at the telescope. APO version.

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_opsdb_apo_configuration.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='fits' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
