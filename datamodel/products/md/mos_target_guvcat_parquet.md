## Basic Information
GALEX unique source catalog from Bianchi et al. 2017 (<a href="https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-4365/aa7053/pdf)">https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/1538-4365/aa7053/pdf)</a>.

For more details on the column descriptions see <a href="https://archive.stsci.edu/hlsp/guvcat/guvcat-column-description">https://archive.stsci.edu/hlsp/guvcat/guvcat-column-description</a>

### Naming Convention
$MOS_TARGET/[V_TARG]/[FTYPE]/mos_guvcat.[FTYPE] with FTYPE='parquet' and V_TARG='2.0.0' for DR20.
